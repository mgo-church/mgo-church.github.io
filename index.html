MGO Church

This app is your all-in-one companion for everything related to Mar Gregorios Orthodox Church. From service timings and prayer resources to community updates and spiritual reflections, stay informed and inspired as you walk your faith journey. Experience the Church like never before—accessible, organized, and always connected.

Events

View and track all parish activities, including Holy Qurbana schedules, feast days, prayer meetings and MGOCSM gatherings. Each event includes time, location, and organizer details for clarity and planning.

Notifications

Receive instant alerts on event updates, last-minute changes, spiritual messages, and important parish announcements—ensuring you never miss a moment that matters.

Navigation

Quickly access your personalized member dashboard with a single tap. View your family profile, contribution history, and service involvements—all from a centralized, easy-to-navigate home screen.
