Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the MGO App (hereby referred to as the “Application”) for mobile devices that was created by MGO Church (hereby referred to as the “Service Provider”) as a free service. The Application is intended for use by registered church members only.

Information Collection and Use

The Application collects limited information solely to support church-related communication, coordination, and member engagement. The Service Provider does not use the Application for marketing, advertising, or commercial purposes.

Information Provided by Members

The Application may store personal information voluntarily provided by members, including but not limited to:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Address or location details provided by the member
  • Family and relationship details
  • Birthdays and wedding anniversaries
  • Vehicle or contact-related details (if provided)

This information is used only for internal church purposes and is not shared for marketing or advertising.

Location Information

The Application does not collect or track real-time device location. Location details used for directions are based solely on address or location information manually provided by members themselves. The Application does not access GPS, background location, or live tracking services.

Analytics and App Usage Data

The Application uses Firebase Analytics to understand general application usage and improve performance.

Firebase Analytics may automatically collect limited, non-personal information such as:

  • App interaction events (for example, screen views)
  • Date and time of access
  • Device and app-related information

The Service Provider does not directly collect, store, or access IP addresses or precise device identifiers.

Google Play Services

The Application uses Google Play Services and Firebase Analytics to support core functionality and analytics. These services may collect limited information in accordance with Google’s Privacy Policy.

Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Data Sharing

The Service Provider does not sell, rent, or share personal data for marketing purposes.

Data may be shared only:

  • Within the church community for internal coordination
  • With trusted service providers such as Firebase Analytics for app analytics
  • When required by law or to protect the safety and rights of users

Account Deletion and Data Retention

Members may request to disable their access to the Application and delete their personal data stored within the Application by contacting the Service Provider at mgochurch.tambarameast@gmail.com.

Upon verification, the Service Provider will remove the member’s digital account and associated data from the Application within a reasonable time.

Please note that deleting the Application account does not automatically remove official church membership records that may be maintained separately for administrative, legal, or historical purposes. Such records, if any, are kept offline and are not publicly accessible.

Opt-Out Rights

You can stop all information collection by the Application by uninstalling it from your mobile device using standard uninstall processes available through your device or application marketplace.

Data Security

The Service Provider takes reasonable measures to protect member information. Access to personal data is restricted to authorized church administrators only.

Children’s Privacy

The Application is intended for church members and families. The Service Provider does not knowingly collect personal data directly from children under the age of 13 without parental or guardian involvement.

Any information related to children is provided by parents or guardians as part of family records. If you believe a child’s data has been included without consent, please contact the Service Provider for removal.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page. Continued use of the Application indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
