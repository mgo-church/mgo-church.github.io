Account & Data Deletion Request – MGO App

If you would like to delete your digital account and associated personal data from the MGO App, please follow the steps below.

How to Request Deletion

Send an email from your registered email address to:

Email: mgochurch.tambarameast@gmail.com
Subject: Account Deletion Request

What Happens Next

  • Our team will verify your identity to protect your account.
  • After verification, all digital data associated with your account stored on our servers for the MGO App (including login details, directory profile, and app settings) will be permanently deleted.
  • You will receive a confirmation email once the process is complete.

Important Note

Deleting your app account removes your digital profile from the mobile application. This does not terminate official MGO Church membership or affect administrative records maintained offline for legal or historical purposes.
